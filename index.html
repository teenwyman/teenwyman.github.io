About Teen

I joined NDORMS as a database programmer in September 2022. I am working on mapping the CPRD data to the OMOP Common Data Model.

My interests include programming and health tech. Before my current position, I worked as an analyst programmer in public and private healthcare sectors in Hong Kong.

Working with a wide range of stakeholders of the Electronic Health Record Sharing System, I assisted in web application development, automated testing implementation, troubleshooting, data analysis, and service enhancement, particularly in drug and immunisation domains. I participated in a clinical management system revamp project for a private hospital, including migrating large-scale medical records and designing and implementing billing and pharmacy dispensing systems.

I completed a Master's degree in Healthcare Operational Management at the University of Warwick and a BSc in Computer Science at the City University of Hong Kong. I have expertise in problem-solving, software development, and health informatics.

My working experience includes healthcare system building, testing, implementation and integration, quality improvement, and project management.
